About our Summit

Code Your Future Women's International Summer Summit is a gathering of women from all walks of life who come together to share ideas, stories and strategies for personal and professional growth.

This year's summit will feature keynote speakers, panel discussions, and workshops on a variety of topics, including leadership, entrepreneurship, education, health and wellness and more.

We aim to provide a holistic support for all the women in our community, by bringing them together from all the regions where we operate, to talk, relax, unwind, learn, support and share stories and experiences in a safe space. We look forward to welcoming a diverse range of women in tech this year, who will be involved in panels discussions, roundtable conversations, presentations and so much more..!
