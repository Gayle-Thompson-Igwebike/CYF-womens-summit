About The Summit:

CYF-WISER is a platform for women from diverse backgrounds to come together, exchange ideas and strategise for personal and professional growth.
This is an empowering gathering that also celebrates the achievements of women who have trained or volunteered at CYF and are now making remarkable contributions to their's and the lives of others.

This year's summit promises an enriching experience with engaging keynote speakers, thought-provoking panel discussions and interactive workshops, covering topics such as leadership, entrepreneurship, education, health and wellness and more.

We want to provide holistic support to all women in our community, fostering a sense of togetherness and creating a safe space for them to share inspiring stories, connect, unwind, learn and support one another.

Join us in celebrating the accomplishments of incredible women, as we continue to foster a supportive and inclusive community in the tech industry.

Together, we are WISER..!!
